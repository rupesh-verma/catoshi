Czatoshi Bridge

Czatoshi Bridge is a bridge service providing access to inter-blockchain liquidity for Binance Smart Chain and Ethereum Chain decentralized applications, and allow user to exchange Czatoshi token from one chain to other. A fixed network fee will be charged according to different networks. The amount differs within different blockchains.

User can easily bridge by connecting to their metamask wallets and filling the amount the want to swap. Once process is complete funds will be deposited in users wallet.
